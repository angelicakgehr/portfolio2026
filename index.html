Compliance Review Redesign

a16z crypto · 2025

The compliance editorial review process was broken: requests scattered across Slack and email, unclear ownership, lost threads, and content being reviewed overnight or on weekends. I rebuilt it from scratch.

What I Built
  • Centralized compliance review workflow in Airtable with automated intake, status tracking, structured email notifications, and live sync to the Compliance team's separate master tracker
  • Email automations that kept all feedback and decisions within a single thread, eliminating scattered comms and reducing friction for the legal team
  • A system complex enough to be robust, but simple enough that every stakeholder could use it day-to-day without training. The best part: no one noticed when I turned it on.
The Result
  • 63% reduction in review turnaround time — from an average of 23 hours to 8.5 hours per review cycle
  • Fewer last-minute review requests and status-chasing loops
  • Editorial and compliance/legal teams operating from the same source of truth for the first time
